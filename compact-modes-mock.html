2 · Minimal mode — pick M1 / M2 / M3

Same interaction in every stage: click pill → radial fan; click a spoke → that option's panel; click background (or the pill again) to close. Everything clamps to the stage edge like the real monitor clamp.

M1 · Ray bloom — panel inflates outward along the clicked spoke's ray

click pill → click any spoke — panel opens on that spoke's side

Menu and content are one gesture: the panel is anchored past the chip you clicked and unclips away from the pill. Other spokes retract. No tab strip — back out to the fan to switch panels.

M2 · Island morph — the pill itself morphs into a mini-panel with icon tabs

click pill → click any spoke — pill rectangle grows into the panel; switch panels via the icon tab strip

Dynamic-Island style: fan retracts, the 36px pill rectangle physically grows into the panel (position+size animate together on the app's curve), content fades in after the morph settles. All five targets stay reachable in the tab header, so switching needs no re-open. Closing reverses the morph back into the pill.

M3 · Petal column — panel unfolds beside the pill; pill stays as the handle

click pill → click any spoke — panel unfolds top-down next to the pill; click the pill to close

Most conservative: pill never disappears, panel reveals top-down on the screen-aware side (right of pill when pill sits in the left half, left otherwise). Tab strip included. Best behavior for edge-parked pills.
